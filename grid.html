SVN college

Nadar Mahajana Sangam S. Vellaichamy Nadar College (NMS SVN College) is an autonomous, co-educational arts and science institution in Nagamalai, Madurai, established in 1965 by the Nadar Mahajana Sangam. Affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University and accredited "A" grade by NAAC, it offers UG, PG, and research programs, with a focus on empowering students from rural areas.

Founder: Dr. P. Vadamalayan
Year Founded: 1965
American college
The American College in Madurai, India, established in 1881 by American missionaries, is a premier, autonomous Christian minority institution affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University. Known for its heritage red-brick campus, it offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs, ranking 54th in NIRF 2024. Arul anandar college
Arul Anandar College (AAC), located in Karumathur, Madurai, is a renowned Jesuit-run, co-educational, autonomous minority institution established in 1970. Affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University, it focuses on rural social transformation, offering diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts, science, and commerce, including its distinct Rural Development Science (RDS) program
Founder: Most Rev. Dr. Justin Diraviam
Year Founded: 1970
